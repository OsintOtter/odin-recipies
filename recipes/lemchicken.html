Lemon Chicken with Sauce

This is a delicious recipe that i have been making for a couple years now. It is refreshing and delicious. Please note that cast iron will be the best for this recipe. Please be careful when butterflying your chicken. That is how I lost the tip of my finger.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs of boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 box of noodles of your choice
  • 3 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 Shallots (Minced)
  • 1 3/4 cup of dry white wine
  • 1 3/4 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 3/4 cup of chicken stock
  • 1 cup of flour
  • Paprika to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Butterly your chicken breasts and pound them out till they are 1/4 inch thick.
  2. Dredge your chicken breasts in a light coating of flour and but them in the HOT lightly oiled cast iron pan
  3. Fry your chicken breasts till golden brown. Once done remove from heat and add your garlic and shallots to the pan. Sear until fragrant
  4. Once fragrant, add white wine and simmer till wine is reduced to half. You should start boiling your water for your pasta at this point.
  5. Add chicken stock and heavy cream to pan, reduce heat to a simmer and add in the juice of 1 lemon.
  6. Cook your pasta and add chicken back into the pan. Let simmer for an additional 5 minutes. Drain your pasta and serve!
